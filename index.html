How It Works

Four simple steps to verify anything before you commit. No more scams, no more regrets.

1

Tell us what you want verified

Submit your verification request with all necessary details. Whether it's a property rental, goods purchase, or service verification, provide us with the specifics and location. Our intelligent system matches your needs with the perfect verifier.

2

Pick from trusted verifiers

Browse our curated network of vetted, local verifiers. Each verifier is thoroughly screened, reviewed, and rated by the community. Choose based on proximity, expertise, ratings, and pricing that fits your budget.

3

Receive photos, videos & voice notes

Get comprehensive multimedia verification delivered directly to your dashboard. Our verifiers provide high-quality photos, detailed videos, and informative voice notes that give you complete confidence in your decision.

4

Confirm or request more

Review the verification and approve if satisfied, or request additional information if needed. Show appreciation for exceptional service by tipping your verifier. Your peace of mind is our priority.
